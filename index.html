Our Services

Qr Guard provides QR code services for vehicles that allow car owners to receive instant alerts about any incidents. By scanning a unique QR code attached to the car, nearby individuals can quickly notify the owner of any issues.

From parking violations to suspicious activities, our service ensures that you stay informed and can respond promptly to any situation involving your vehicle.

How It Works

Step 1: Register Your Vehicle

Create an account with us and register your vehicle. A unique QR code will be generated specifically for your car.

Step 2: Place the QR Code on Your Vehicle

Receive your QR code decal, which is durable and easily scannable. Place it on your car where it’s visible to passersby.

Step 3: Receive Instant Alerts

When someone scans the QR code, they will be able to send you alerts for various issues related to your vehicle.

Alert Options

Our service provides the following types of alerts to keep you informed of any situation involving your vehicle:

  • Vehicle in Trouble: Notifies the owner if there’s a major mechanical issue or breakdown.
  • Parked in No Parking Zone: Alerts you if your vehicle is parked illegally.
  • Child or Pet Left Inside: Critical alert if a child or pet is locked inside the vehicle.
  • Suspicious Activity Near Vehicle: Warning of potential vandalism or suspicious behavior near your car.
  • Car Blocking Exit: Notification if your vehicle is obstructing a path or route.
  • Vehicle Fire: Emergency alert in case of fire within or near the vehicle.
  • Fuel/Fluid Leak: Alerts you if there are signs of fuel or other fluid leaks under the vehicle.
