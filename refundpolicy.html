Refund Policy

At QRGuard, we strive to provide the best products and services to our customers. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward refund process. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • The product or service is defective or not as described.
  • You have canceled your subscription within the allowed trial period (if applicable).
  • The purchase was made in error, and you notify us within 7 days of the transaction.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Customizable QR Codes that have already been generated and delivered.
  • Subscription plans after the trial period has ended.
  • Services that have already been fully utilized (e.g., completed emergency assistance requests).

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at qrguardprivatelimited@gmail.com within 7 days of your purchase.
  2. Provide your order details, including the transaction ID and reason for the refund request.
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days.

4. Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method used during the purchase.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed.

Note: If you do not receive your refund within the specified time frame, please contact us immediately at support@qrguard.com.

5. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. However, cancellations made after the trial period will not be eligible for a refund. To cancel, log in to your account and follow the cancellation instructions or contact our support team.
